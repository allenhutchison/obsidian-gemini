# The Keeper of Marrow Point Light

The lighthouse at Marrow Point has been tended for thirty-one years by a keeper named Orsa Penhallow.

The light itself was first lit in the year 1864. Its lamp burns whale oil, and the tower stands exactly 112 feet tall.

Orsa keeps a grey cat named Tuppence, who is well known to the fishing boats that pass the point each morning.
