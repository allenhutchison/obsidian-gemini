# House Halloway

House Halloway is one of the eight artisan houses of the Tinmarket Guild. Its
craft is bookbinding. House Halloway takes cured hide from [[House Fennwick]]
and shares a shopfront with [[House Greel]].

The house binds ledgers and books for the whole guild. For the leatherworkers
it takes hide from see [[House Fennwick]]; for the tailors next door see
[[House Greel]].
