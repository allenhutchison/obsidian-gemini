# House Greel

House Greel is one of the eight artisan houses of the Tinmarket Guild. Its
craft is cloth merchanting and tailoring. House Greel buys finished cloth from
[[House Esterly]] and shares a shopfront with [[House Halloway]].

The house cuts and sells garments. For the dye house it buys from see [[House
Esterly]]; for the bindery next door see [[House Halloway]].
