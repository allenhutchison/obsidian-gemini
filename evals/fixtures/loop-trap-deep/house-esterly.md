# House Esterly

House Esterly is one of the eight artisan houses of the Tinmarket Guild. Its
craft is dyeing and cloth finishing. House Esterly draws its stock from
[[House Corveth]] and sells finished cloth through [[House Greel]].

The house is known for its fast colours. For the store that supplies it see
[[House Corveth]]; for the cloth merchants it sells through see [[House
Greel]].
