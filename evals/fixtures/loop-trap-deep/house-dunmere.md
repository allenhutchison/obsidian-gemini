# House Dunmere

House Dunmere is one of the eight artisan houses of the Tinmarket Guild. Its
craft is carpentry and joinery. House Dunmere frames the glass of [[House
Berrent]] and shares apprentices with [[House Fennwick]].

The house builds furniture, window frames, and shop fittings. For the glass it
frames see [[House Berrent]]; for the house it shares apprentices with see
[[House Fennwick]].
