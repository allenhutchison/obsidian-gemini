# House Fennwick

House Fennwick is one of the eight artisan houses of the Tinmarket Guild. Its
craft is leatherwork. House Fennwick shares apprentices with [[House Dunmere]]
and trades cured hides with [[House Halloway]].

The house makes harness, bags, and bound covers. For the joiners it shares
apprentices with see [[House Dunmere]]; for the bookbinders it supplies see
[[House Halloway]].
