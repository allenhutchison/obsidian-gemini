# House Corveth

House Corveth is one of the eight artisan houses of the Tinmarket Guild. Its
craft is the supply and storage of raw stock for the other houses. House
Corveth provisions [[House Aldwin]] and works closely with [[House Esterly]].

The house holds the guild's main store and its weighing room. For the
metalworkers it supplies see [[House Aldwin]]; for the dye house it provisions
see [[House Esterly]].
