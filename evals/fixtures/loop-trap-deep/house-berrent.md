# House Berrent

House Berrent is one of the eight artisan houses of the Tinmarket Guild. Its
craft is glasswork. House Berrent stands next to [[House Aldwin]] and supplies
finished panes to [[House Dunmere]].

The house is known for its window glass and lamp chimneys. For neighbouring
metalwork see [[House Aldwin]]; for the carpentry that frames its glass see
[[House Dunmere]].
