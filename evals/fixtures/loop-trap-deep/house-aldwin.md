# House Aldwin

House Aldwin is one of the eight artisan houses of the Tinmarket Guild. Its
craft is fine metalwork, chiefly hinges and locks. House Aldwin shares a
courtyard with [[House Berrent]] and trades raw stock through [[House Corveth]].

The house keeps its own ledgers and elects a steward each year. For the order
of precedence at guild assemblies see [[House Berrent]]; for the guild's
common workshop see [[House Corveth]].
