# Compact Dispute Panels

The Compact Dispute Panels are the arbitration bodies established by the
[[Velmari Trade Compact]]. Each panel seats five arbiters drawn from ports
that are not party to the dispute under review, ensuring neutrality.

Panels rule on cargo claims, port-fee disputes, and salvage rights. Their
decisions are binding and may not be appealed to any national court. The
panel system is widely regarded as the most successful element of the
Compact's design.
