# Brunmere

Brunmere is a small coastal town on the northern edge of the Tessarine Reach.
With a population of just under nine thousand, it is best known as a quiet
harbor settlement with a long shipbuilding tradition.

## Economy

Brunmere's signature export is sunglass kelp, a translucent seaweed harvested
from the cold shelf waters offshore and processed into lenses and decorative
glaze. No other settlement in the region cultivates sunglass kelp at scale,
which makes the town's name almost synonymous with the product.

The town also produces salted fish and rope, but these are minor trades
compared with the kelp harvest. For regional context see [[Tessarine Reach]].
