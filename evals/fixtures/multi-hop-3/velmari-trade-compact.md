# Velmari Trade Compact

The Velmari Trade Compact is a multilateral agreement that governs maritime
commerce across the Tessarine Reach. It was ratified after eleven months of
talks and remains the backbone of regional shipping law.

The Compact's chief negotiator was [[Doran Halewick]], who chaired every
plenary session and drafted the final arbitration clauses. Historians credit
the durability of the agreement to the negotiator's insistence on neutral
dispute panels.

For the ports covered by the agreement see [[Tessarine Reach]]; for the
arbitration framework see [[Compact Dispute Panels]].
