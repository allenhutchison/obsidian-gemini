# Tessarine Reach

The Tessarine Reach is a broad coastal region of sheltered bays and trading
ports. It hosts more than forty registered harbors, of which the largest are
Caldreth Port, Olwen Bay, and the deepwater terminal at Greyfen.

The Reach has been a center of maritime commerce for centuries, and its
shipping lanes are now governed by the [[Velmari Trade Compact]]. Smaller
harbor towns such as [[Brunmere]] specialize in regional exports rather than
bulk freight.
