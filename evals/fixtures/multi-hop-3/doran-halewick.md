# Doran Halewick

Doran Halewick was a diplomat and maritime lawyer best known for chairing the
talks behind the [[Velmari Trade Compact]]. Halewick spent three decades in
the arbitration service before retiring to private practice.

## Early Life

Halewick was born and raised in the coastal town of [[Brunmere]], where the
family ran a small chandlery near the harbor. Halewick often said that growing
up around shipping ledgers shaped a lifelong interest in trade law.

After secondary schooling, Halewick studied law at the inland city of
Caldreth before returning to the coast to practice.
