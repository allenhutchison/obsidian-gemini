# Security Policy

This policy covers physical and information security at Meridian.

Access to the Aurora Vault test facility is limited to badged engineering staff. Flight hardware in the Vault must be logged in and out. Visitors require an escort at all times.

Project documentation is classified at two levels: internal and restricted. Restricted documents include the avionics firmware source for [[Project Cinder]] and the propulsion designs for [[Project Garnet]].

All staff complete annual security training. This policy is maintained by quality lead [[Helena Voss]] and is reviewed each January.
