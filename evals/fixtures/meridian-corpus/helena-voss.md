# Helena Voss

Helena Voss is Meridian's quality lead. She does not own any project; instead she runs quality assurance and qualification oversight across the entire portfolio.

Voss joined Meridian in 2020. Before Meridian, she spent eleven years at [[Northwind Dynamics]], where she led the quality-assurance organization for that company's flight programs.

Within Meridian, Voss has mentored several engineers. Most notably, she is the mentor of [[Priya Raman]], whom she coached through the transition into program integration.

Voss attends the [[Halcyon Design Review]] and the [[Ground Software Review]] meetings as the standing quality representative. She holds a degree in industrial engineering and is known for her rigorous audit checklists.
