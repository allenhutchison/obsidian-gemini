# Priya Raman

Priya Raman is Meridian's head of program integration and the acting head of the lunar program. She owns three projects: the flagship orbital tug [[Project Halcyon]], the communications relay [[Project Beacon]], and the planned lunar lander [[Project Vesper]].

Raman joined Meridian in 2020 as a systems engineer and was promoted to integration lead in 2023. Within Meridian, her mentor and the person she credits with shaping her systems-engineering approach is [[Helena Voss]], Meridian's quality lead.

Before Meridian, Raman spent six years as a guidance engineer on commercial launch vehicles. She holds a master's degree in aerospace engineering.

Raman chairs the [[Halcyon Design Review]] and attends the [[Robotics Sync]] and [[Ground Software Review]] meetings. Her office is in the Cascade Falls headquarters building.
