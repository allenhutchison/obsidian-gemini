# Propulsion Stand Review

Review of the [[Project Garnet]] propulsion test stand, held 10 March 2026 and chaired by Tomas Reyes.

Attendees: Tomas Reyes, Dana Okoro, Sofia Lindqvist.

## Topics

Tomas Reyes presented the test-stand layout for the green-propellant thruster, targeting 440 newtons of vacuum thrust. Dana Okoro confirmed that the [[Project Aster]] star tracker would supply the precise pointing the stand needs. Sofia Lindqvist outlined the data-acquisition software the stand will use.

The team noted Garnet remains in the planning phase with no test article fabricated.

## Actions

Tomas Reyes to finalize the stand structural drawings. Dana Okoro to deliver the Aster interface document.
