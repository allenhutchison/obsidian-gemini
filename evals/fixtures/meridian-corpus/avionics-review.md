# Avionics Review

Avionics review for [[Project Cinder]], held 8 May 2026 and chaired by Dana Okoro.

Attendees: Dana Okoro, Marcus Feld, Tomas Reyes.

## Topics

Dana Okoro walked through the Cinder firmware qualification status. The team confirmed that the Cinder core meets its radiation-tolerance targets. Marcus Feld raised motor-control timing questions for the Talos arm. Tomas Reyes asked about Cinder integration for the Garnet test stand.

A schedule risk was logged: any slip on Cinder cascades to [[Project Talos]], [[Project Beacon]], and [[Project Aster]].
