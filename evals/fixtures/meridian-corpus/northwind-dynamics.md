# Northwind Dynamics

Northwind Dynamics was a small experimental-aircraft company that operated from 2006 to 2018. Several Meridian employees, including quality lead [[Helena Voss]], began their careers there.

Northwind's flagship effort was the [[Skylark Testbed]], an uncrewed experimental aircraft used to prove out fly-by-wire control laws. The Skylark program defined Northwind's reputation.

Northwind never built a second aircraft type. After the Skylark program wound down, the company was dissolved in 2018 and most of its engineering staff dispersed to other firms.

Northwind Dynamics was based in Boise, Idaho. It is unrelated to the similarly named [[Northwind Robotics]], a separate supplier that Meridian still uses for actuator parts.
