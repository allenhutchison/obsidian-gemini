# Skylark Relay

The Skylark Relay is a commercial communications product, unrelated to the [[Skylark Testbed]] experimental aircraft despite the shared name.

The Skylark Relay is a ground-station data router sold by a third-party vendor. Meridian evaluated it for the [[Project Beacon]] ground segment but did not adopt it.

The Skylark Relay has no call sign, no aircraft, and no connection to [[Northwind Dynamics]]. It is purely a piece of network hardware.

This note exists only to disambiguate the name "Skylark" from the historical Skylark Testbed aircraft program.
