# Procurement Policy

This policy covers how Meridian projects purchase parts and services.

Any single purchase above 50,000 dollars requires sign-off from the project owner and from quality lead [[Helena Voss]]. Purchases above 250,000 dollars additionally require program-integration approval from [[Priya Raman]].

Long-lead flight hardware must be ordered at least nine months before the project `due` date. Actuator parts are sourced from the supplier [[Northwind Robotics]], which is unrelated to the defunct aircraft company [[Northwind Dynamics]].

All procurement records are retained for seven years. This policy is owned by Priya Raman.
