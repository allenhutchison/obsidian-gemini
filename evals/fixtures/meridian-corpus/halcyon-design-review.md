# Halcyon Design Review

Design review for [[Project Halcyon]], held 20 April 2026 and chaired by Priya Raman.

Attendees: Priya Raman, Marcus Feld, Dana Okoro, Helena Voss.

## Topics

The review covered the capture-arm interface from [[Project Talos]] and the relay interface from [[Project Beacon]]. Marcus Feld presented updated structural margins. Dana Okoro confirmed the Cinder avionics integration plan.

During qualification testing a power dropout was investigated, and the anomaly was traced to a thermal-grease void in connector J7. A rework of the connector batch was approved.

## Actions

Helena Voss to issue a quality audit of the connector supplier. Priya Raman to update the Halcyon schedule. The review board cleared Halcyon to proceed to the build phase.
