# Northwind Robotics

Northwind Robotics is a current parts supplier to Meridian. It manufactures the actuators and harmonic drives used in robotic mechanisms.

Despite the similar name, Northwind Robotics has no connection to [[Northwind Dynamics]], the defunct experimental-aircraft company. The two firms simply share a common geographic name and were founded by unrelated people.

Northwind Robotics supplies harmonic drives for the [[Project Talos]] capture arm. It has been a Meridian vendor since 2021 and is approved under the [[Procurement Policy]].

Northwind Robotics is based in Spokane, Washington. It has never built an aircraft and has no test-aircraft program of any kind.
