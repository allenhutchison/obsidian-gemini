# Sofia Lindqvist

Sofia Lindqvist leads mission software and operations at Meridian. She owns the ground software suite [[Project Quill]] and was the owner of the archived cubesat swarm [[Project Drift]].

Lindqvist joined Meridian in 2022 after a decade writing flight and ground software for small-satellite operators. She drove the decision to write the Quill codebase in Rust.

Her team runs the Meridian control room and is responsible for every operator-facing tool. She also coordinates operations training for new console staff.

Lindqvist chairs the [[Ground Software Review]] and attends the [[Propulsion Stand Review]] meeting. She holds a degree in computer science and is based in the Cascade Falls headquarters.
