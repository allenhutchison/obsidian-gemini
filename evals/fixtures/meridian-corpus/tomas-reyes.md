# Tomas Reyes

Tomas Reyes is Meridian's propulsion lead. He owns the planned propulsion test program [[Project Garnet]], the company's largest-budget project.

Reyes joined Meridian in 2023 to stand up an in-house propulsion capability. Before that he spent nine years on green-propellant thruster development at a research institute.

Reyes is responsible for the Garnet test stand and the small green-propellant thruster it will qualify. He works closely with Dana Okoro because the test stand relies on the Aster star tracker for pointing.

Reyes attends the [[Avionics Review]] and chairs the [[Propulsion Stand Review]] meeting. He holds a master's degree in propulsion engineering and spends most of his time at the Aurora Vault test facility.
