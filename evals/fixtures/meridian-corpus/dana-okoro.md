# Dana Okoro

Dana Okoro leads avionics and guidance at Meridian. Okoro owns two projects: the shared avionics core [[Project Cinder]] and the star tracker [[Project Aster]].

Okoro joined Meridian in 2021 from a defense electronics firm, where she designed radiation-tolerant flight computers. That background directly shaped the Cinder core.

Because Cinder underpins nearly every flight system at Meridian, Okoro coordinates closely with most other project owners. She is the company's authority on flight-software qualification.

Okoro chairs the [[Avionics Review]], chairs the [[Guidance Sync]], and attends the [[Halcyon Design Review]] and the [[Propulsion Stand Review]] meetings. She holds a doctorate in electrical engineering.
