# Marcus Feld

Marcus Feld leads robotics and structures at Meridian. He owns the robotic-arm program [[Project Talos]] and was the owner of the now-archived thermal shield program [[Project Onyx]].

Feld joined Meridian in 2019 as one of the founding engineers. He previously spent eight years building industrial manipulators in the automotive sector.

Feld's teams handle every moving mechanical part at Meridian, from the Talos capture arm to deployment mechanisms. He is known internally for insisting on extra structural margin.

Feld attends the [[Halcyon Design Review]], the [[Avionics Review]], and chairs the [[Robotics Sync]] meeting. He holds a degree in mechanical engineering and works out of the Aurora Vault test facility most days.
