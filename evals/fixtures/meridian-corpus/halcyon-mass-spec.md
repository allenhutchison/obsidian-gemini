# Halcyon Mass Spec

This is the controlled mass specification for [[Project Halcyon]].

After the connector rework approved at the [[Halcyon Design Review]], the Halcyon team re-ran the mass rollup. The current controlled value is a vehicle dry mass of 1,240 kilograms.

This figure supersedes any earlier dry-mass number stated in the [[Project Halcyon]] overview note, which was written before the rework. Per the [[Conflict Resolution Policy]] recency rule, the more recently updated note is the one to trust.

Propellant load and payload allocation are tracked separately and are not part of the dry-mass figure.
