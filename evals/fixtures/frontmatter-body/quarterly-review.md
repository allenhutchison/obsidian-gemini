# Quarterly Review

The Driftwood support team handled a record number of tickets this quarter while
keeping the median first-response time under four hours. Two new agents finished
onboarding and are now taking the full ticket queue.

The biggest pain point was the billing escalation path, which routed too many
simple refund requests to senior staff. A revised routing rule shipped in the
last week of the quarter and early numbers look promising.

Looking ahead, the team wants to invest in a self-service knowledge base to cut
repeat questions about password resets and plan changes.
