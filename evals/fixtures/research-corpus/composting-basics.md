# Composting Basics

Composting at the Mossfield community garden turns kitchen scraps and yard waste
into a dark, crumbly soil amendment. A healthy pile needs a balance of greens and
browns: greens supply nitrogen, browns supply carbon.

Greens at Mossfield include vegetable peelings, coffee grounds, and fresh grass
clippings. Browns include dried leaves, shredded cardboard, and straw. The garden
crew aims for roughly three parts brown to one part green by volume.
