# Harbor Master Notes

These are the working notes of the Quill Harbor harbor master, covering the
day-to-day running of the port.

## Berthing

Berths on the inner jetty are assigned by the harbor master at the start of
each season. Priority goes to vessels that land at the fish market daily. The
outer breakwater berths are reserved for visiting boats and the lifeboat.

## Dues and Fees

Harbor dues are collected monthly. The schedule is posted at the market and is
set each year by the harbor committee. Visiting boats pay a daily rate at the
harbor master's office.

## Safety

The harbor master maintains the lifeboat roster and tests the harbor lights
weekly. All skippers are expected to report unsafe conditions promptly.
