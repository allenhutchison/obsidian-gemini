# Cordwain Spice Guild — salt quotas

This note records routine business of the Cordwain Spice Guild concerning salt quotas.

The guild has operated from the river district for many years and keeps careful records of every transaction. Members meet each month to review accounts, settle disputes, and plan the coming season's trade. Nothing in this note concerns the location of the master ledger.

Entry number 13 in the guild's general archive.
