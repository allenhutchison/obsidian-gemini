# Onboarding Guide

This guide explains how a new member of the Greenways Trail Crew gets started.

## First Week

A new crew member is paired with a mentor for the first week. The mentor walks
them through the tool shed inventory, the signing-out procedure, and the
trail-map filing system. By the end of the week the new member should be able
to sign out tools and locate any trail by its map reference.

## Safety Briefing

Before joining any work party, every new member completes the safety briefing.
It covers tool handling, what to do if someone is injured on the trail, and
the radio call-in schedule. The briefing is repeated for all crew once a year.

## Equipment

New members are issued a helmet, gloves, and a marked work vest. Larger tools
remain shared and are signed out from the shed for each work party.
