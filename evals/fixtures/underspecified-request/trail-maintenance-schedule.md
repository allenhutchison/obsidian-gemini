# Trail Maintenance Schedule

This document sets out the seasonal maintenance schedule for the Greenways
trail network.

## Spring

In spring the crew clears winter deadfall, repairs drainage channels, and
re-marks any waymarks faded by the weather. Spring work is the heaviest of the
year and usually needs every available crew member.

## Summer

Summer work is lighter: vegetation cutting along the trail edges and minor
surface repair. The summer schedule is built around volunteer availability and
is reviewed each month.

## Autumn and Winter

Autumn work focuses on clearing leaf fall from drainage channels before the
first frost. Winter work is limited to emergency repairs only, since frozen
ground makes most maintenance impractical.
