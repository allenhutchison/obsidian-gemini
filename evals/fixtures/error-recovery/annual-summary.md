# Annual Summary

This note gives a one-page overview of the Brindlewood Community Orchard's
year, drawing the quarterly reports together.

## The Year in Brief

The orchard planted new trees in the spring, came through the blossom season
with careful thinning, lost one mature pear tree to a summer storm, and
finished with a strong autumn harvest.

## Volunteer Total

Across the year the orchard relied on a large body of volunteers, and the
quarterly reports each record the hours logged in their own period.

## Outlook

The orchard committee judged the year a success and set its planting plan for
the following spring.
