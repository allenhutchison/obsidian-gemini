# Quarterly Report Q1

This is the first-quarter report for the Brindlewood Community Orchard.

## Plantings

In the first quarter the orchard crew planted eighteen new apple whips along
the eastern fence and replaced four trees lost to the winter. The new whips
are all of the Marrow Pippin variety.

## Volunteers

The orchard logged two hundred and ten volunteer hours in the quarter, spread
across nine work mornings.

## Finances

First-quarter spending was modest: tools, tree stock, and fence repair. The
orchard ended the quarter with a small surplus carried forward.
