# Quarterly Report Q2

This is the second-quarter report for the Brindlewood Community Orchard.

## Blossom and Thinning

The second quarter covered the blossom season. The crew thinned the young
trees by hand to protect them from over-cropping, and the bees from the
neighbouring apiary worked the orchard through the blossom.

## Volunteers

The orchard logged three hundred and forty volunteer hours in the quarter, the
busiest period of the year so far.

## The Storm

A summer storm in the second quarter brought down one mature pear tree. The
crew cleared it and milled the timber for orchard benches.
