# Quarterly Report Q3

This is the third-quarter report for the Brindlewood Community Orchard.

## Harvest

The third quarter is the harvest. The orchard brought in a recorded harvest of
nine hundred and forty kilograms of apples, the largest harvest in the
orchard's history. Most of the crop was the Marrow Pippin variety.

## Volunteers

The orchard logged two hundred and eighty volunteer hours in the quarter, most
of it during the three harvest weekends.

## Distribution

The harvested apples were shared among volunteer households, donated to the
local food bank, and pressed into juice at the community press.
