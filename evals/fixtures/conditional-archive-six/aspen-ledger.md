# Aspen Ledger

Aspen Ledger was a finance project to reconcile the old paper accounts of the
Aspen Hollow trust. The reconciliation finished and the final statement was
signed off well before the present day. No further work is planned and the
working files have been packed away in long-term storage.

Its end_date is firmly in the past.
