# Pelmont Civic Museum

The Pelmont Civic Museum is the principal museum of the town of Pelmont. It
occupies a converted assembly hall near the town square and is open to the
public six days a week.

## Collections

The museum's collection spans local history, natural history, and a small
gallery of mechanical curiosities, of which the [[Orrery of Pelmont]] is the
centerpiece. The natural history room holds mounted birds and a case of
mineral specimens; the local history room displays tools, documents, and
photographs of old Pelmont.

## Building

The assembly hall was given to the town and converted into a museum. The great
hall, where the orrery stands, keeps its original timber roof. The smaller
collection rooms were partitioned from the former side aisles.

## Visiting

Admission is free, supported by the town and by a small membership society.
The museum runs guided tours on weekend mornings and school visits by
arrangement. A printed gallery guide is available at the entrance desk.
