# Onboarding Guide for the Tidewater Field Team

## Overview

This guide explains how a new field technician gets set up at Tidewater Survey
Co. Read it on your first day and keep it handy for the first month.

## Equipment Pickup

When you arrive on your first day, you should make your way over to the gear
cage, which is the room located at the far end of the basement hallway past the
break room, and there you will need to speak with the equipment steward, whose
name is Marcy, and she will, after checking your name against the roster that
the office manager prepared, hand over to you a field tablet, a measuring kit,
and a high-visibility vest, and you should be aware that each of these items is
tagged with a barcode and that you are personally responsible for every one of
them until the day that you eventually return them all back to the gear cage.

## Safety Briefing

Before your first site visit you must complete the safety briefing with your
crew lead. It covers terrain hazards, radio check-in procedure, and what to do
if you lose contact with the crew. The briefing takes about thirty minutes.

## First Week Checklist

- Pick up your equipment from the gear cage.
- Complete the safety briefing with your crew lead.
- Shadow an experienced technician on at least two site visits.
- Submit your first practice survey for review.
