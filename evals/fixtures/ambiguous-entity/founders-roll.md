# Founders' Roll of the Verth Institute

The Verth Institute records two members of the Tarrowin family among its
founding generation. They were not closely related and worked in different
departments, and the Institute archive keeps their files strictly separate to
prevent the confusion that their shared surname has caused over the years.

## Director Aldous Tarrowin

Aldous Tarrowin was the Institute's first administrative director. He never
held a research post; his work was entirely in governance, finance, and the
construction of the original Institute buildings. He served as director for
twenty-two years and is remembered for securing the Institute's permanent
endowment.

## Researcher Lirien Tarrowin

Lirien Tarrowin was a founding research fellow in the Institute's optics
department. She held no administrative office and is remembered entirely for
her scientific work. Her early papers on lens grinding are still cited in the
department's teaching notes.
