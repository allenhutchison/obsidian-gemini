# Harrowgate Observatory

Harrowgate Observatory is a mountaintop research facility famous for its
twin reflecting telescopes. It was commissioned to support long-baseline
sky surveys and remains in continuous operation.

The Observatory's data feeds several regional projects. For its instrument
catalog see [[Telescope Array Specifications]]; for staffing history see
[[Observatory Directors]]. The facility also collaborates closely with the
nearby [[Caldreth Weather Station]].

Note that the Observatory links outward to those three notes; it does not
itself link back to the projects that depend on its data.
