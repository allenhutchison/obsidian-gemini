# Astronomer Pell Rourke

Pell Rourke is an observational astronomer specializing in variable stars.
Rourke spent fourteen years on the staff of [[Harrowgate Observatory]],
where the twin reflectors made faint-star monitoring possible.

Rourke later moved into teaching but still publishes light-curve studies
based on archival plates. Colleagues describe Rourke as meticulous about
calibration and famously skeptical of unconfirmed detections.
